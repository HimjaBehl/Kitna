Build a **full-stack production-style MVP** web app called **Kitna**.

## Goal

Kitna is a money clarity dashboard for creators and influencer managers.

It helps users answer:

* how much work they booked
* how much money actually came in
* how much is still pending
* how much they spent
* how much they actually kept

This is not a mockup or prototype.
Build it as a real full-stack app with:

* working frontend
* working backend
* PostgreSQL database
* Prisma ORM
* auth
* CRUD operations
* seeded sample data
* dashboard calculations

## Product structure

### Core entities

1. Users
2. Deals
3. Payments
4. Expenses
5. Activity logs

### Core logic

* A deal = expected/booked money
* A payment = actual money received
* A single deal can have multiple payments
* An expense may optionally be linked to a deal
* Receivables = unpaid amount from a deal
* Net profit = total received minus total expenses

## Dashboard calculations

Calculate dynamically:

* Total Booked = sum of amount_agreed across non-barter deals
* Total Received = sum of all payments
* Pending Receivables = total unpaid across deals
* Total Expenses = sum of all expenses
* Net Profit = total received minus total expenses
* Potential Profit = total booked minus total expenses

### Deal status rules

* no payment yet + due date future = pending_payment
* partial payment received = partially_paid
* full payment received = paid
* unpaid after due date = overdue
