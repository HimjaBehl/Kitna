# 💸 Kitna — Creator Deal & Payment Tracker

A financial tracking tool built for Indian creators and influencers to finally answer the question: *"Kitna banega?"*

## 🎯 The Problem

Creators juggle 10+ brand deals at a time — across Instagram, YouTube, podcasts. Payments come in via UPI, bank transfer, cheques, at different times, from different brands. There's no single place to see what's been paid, what's pending, and what's overdue.

Excel sheets break. Memory fails. Money gets forgotten.

## ✨ What Kitna Does

- **Deal tracker** — log every brand collaboration with amount, deliverable, and deadline
- **Payment status** — mark deals as paid / pending / overdue at a glance
- **Payment mode tracking** — UPI, bank transfer, or other
- **Revenue dashboard** — see total earned, total pending, and total overdue in one view
- **Bank statement reconciliation** — cross-check what came in vs. what was promised

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript (single-page web app)
- **Platform:** Replit
- **Deployment:** Replit hosting

## 💡 Why I Built This

Influencer marketing in India is a ₹2,000+ crore industry — but most creators are still tracking their income in WhatsApp notes. Kitna is a zero-friction tool that works the way creators actually work: mobile-first, simple, and fast.
